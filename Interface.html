GET YOUR DATA CLEANED!!!




CleanUp is an automated data cleaning software. Here you get various options for cleaning up your data, viz. Removing Outliers, Filling null values, Removing duplicates, Remove extra spaces and format names; dates. CleanUp always keeps your data private on your own computer until YOU want to share or collaborate. Your private data never leaves your computer unless you want it to.


OUR SERVICES

Remove Outliers

Removes the data points that differ significantly from other observations.

Fill Out Null Values

Removes and fills out the null values detected in the dataset.

Remove Duplicate Values

Removes all duplicacies in values detected in the dataset

Remove Extra Spaces

Removes the white blank spaces detected in the dataset

Format Names, Dates

Formats Names and Dates in the correct format in dataset
